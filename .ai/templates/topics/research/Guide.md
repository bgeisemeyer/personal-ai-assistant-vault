# Research Guide

## Purpose
- Maintain research topics in a traceable, AI-readable way.

## Required Files
- `Index.md`
- `Log.md`
- `Questions.md`
- `Insights.md`
- `Sources.md`

## Workflow
1. Read `Index.md`.
2. Read the latest entries in `Log.md`.
3. Add or reference the active question in `Questions.md`.
4. Document work in `Log.md`.
5. Add distilled results to `Insights.md`.
6. Record sources in `Sources.md`.
7. Update status and next steps in `Index.md`.
