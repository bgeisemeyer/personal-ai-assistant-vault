# Research Skill

## Activation
- New research topics
- Ongoing research topics
- Summaries or distillation of research findings

## Workflow
1. Apply `.ai/governance/`.
2. Read the local `Index.md`.
3. Read the latest `Log.md` entries.
4. Maintain `Questions.md`, `Insights.md`, and `Sources.md`.
5. Update status and next steps in `Index.md`.

## Required Structure
- `Index.md`
- `Log.md`
- `Questions.md`
- `Insights.md`
- `Sources.md`

## Quality Criteria
- No open question without status.
- No decision without evidence.
- No source without note or usage trace.
- No changes without checking affected links.
